Classic Stylez

Artist/ Songwriter

A native of the Queen City and a true force to be reckoned within the music game, Classic Stylez has proven time and time again that the true definition of hip hop never lies within gimmicks or compromising the art for fame but instead within the true talent of the artist and their passion for the hip hop movement. An instantaneous freestyle King known to spit ridiculously crazy bars at the drop of the hat, it's no secret that Classic is ready to show the world that he is a cut above the rest. Rounding out his Artist Development trilogy, many are already coining his work on his upcoming project #AD3 (Artist Development 3) LEGENDARY.
